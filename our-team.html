Jonathan LaBoube, CPA
Principal & Founder

Jonathan founded Optimize on the belief that the best advisory work is delivered by people who have held the title, carried the weight, and earned the perspective they're being paid to share. With a background spanning financial leadership, M&A, and helping scale an oil and gas startup to more than $200 million in annual revenue, he leads every engagement with firsthand operating experience and a commitment to results that are practical, not theoretical.
