Our Story

How We Started

Built Through Experience. Not Theory.

Optimize Strategic Advisors was founded on a simple observation: many growing businesses face increasingly complex challenges but lack access to experienced executive leadership when they need it most. Business owners are often forced to choose between hiring expensive full-time executives or relying on advisors who have never operated a business from the inside. We believed there was a better option.

After spending nearly two decades in accounting, finance, operations, and strategic leadership roles, our founder experienced firsthand the challenges that accompany growth. From leading financial operations and supporting mergers and acquisitions to helping scale an oil and gas startup from its early stages to more than $200 million in annual revenue, those experiences shaped the foundation of Optimize. The firm's service offerings evolved around the areas where business leaders needed the most support: financial leadership, sales performance, organizational effectiveness, and executive development.

Today, Optimize partners directly with business owners and leadership teams to navigate complexity, improve performance, and create sustainable growth. Every engagement is guided by practical experience, objective insight, and a commitment to helping clients move from potential to performance.
