Our Services

Principal-led engagements across three integrated disciplines.

Service 01

Fractional CFO

Most growing businesses reach a point where they need senior financial leadership — but aren't ready, or don't need, a full-time CFO. Optimize provides experienced, principal-led financial oversight that gives business owners and leadership teams the visibility, strategy, and confidence to make better decisions without the overhead of a full-time executive hire. We specialize in the moments that matter most: growth inflection points, pending transactions, and decisions that shape the long-term trajectory of your business.

  • Cash flow management and working capital optimization
  • Budgeting, forecasting, and scenario modeling
  • Financial reporting, analysis, and KPI development
  • M&A due diligence and quality of earnings support
  • Bank covenant compliance and lender relationship management
  • Capital deployment strategy
  • New product launches and go-to-market financial planning
  • Consumer market analysis and customer profiling
  • Interim CFO services

Service 02

Commercial Excellence

Sales performance and commercial strategy are disciplines — not personality traits. Optimize works with sales leaders, commercial teams, and executive sponsors to build the systems, structure, and accountability frameworks that drive consistent, measurable revenue growth. From sales culture transformation to go-to-market planning, we help organizations move beyond potential and execute at a higher level.

  • Sales team performance coaching and workshop facilitation
  • Sales executive and commercial leadership advisory
  • Sales culture development and systematization
  • Go-to-market strategy and commercial planning
  • Revenue growth and pricing optimization
  • Sales organization design and performance management
  • Market research and competitive analysis

Service 03

Organizational & Growth Advisory Services

Strong financials and strong sales only get a company so far — what determines whether growth holds is the organization underneath it. Optimize works with leadership teams on the strategic, cultural, and structural work that growth and transition demand: aligning people and process, preparing for what's next, and building an organization that can scale without losing what makes it work.

  • Strategic planning and growth roadmaps
  • Culture and communication alignment
  • Talent development and succession planning
  • Change management through growth or transition
  • Organizational design and structure
  • Leadership pipeline and team development
  • Transaction advisory and deal readiness
  • Post-transaction integration support
