Our Services

Principal-led engagements across three integrated disciplines.

Service 01

Fractional CFO

Most growing businesses reach a point where they need senior financial leadership — but aren't ready, or don't need, a full-time CFO. Optimize provides experienced, principal-led financial oversight that gives business owners and leadership teams the visibility, strategy, and confidence to make better decisions without the overhead of a full-time executive hire. We specialize in the moments that matter most: growth inflection points, pending transactions, and decisions that shape the long-term trajectory of your business.

  • Cash flow management and working capital optimization
  • Budgeting, forecasting, and scenario modeling
  • Financial reporting, analysis, and KPI development
  • M&A due diligence and quality of earnings support
  • Bank covenant compliance and lender relationship management
  • Capital deployment strategy
  • New product launches and go-to-market financial planning
  • Consumer market analysis and customer profiling
  • Interim CFO services

Service 02

Executive Coaching

Leadership at the senior level is rarely about capability — it's about clarity, alignment, and the ability to navigate complexity without losing perspective. Optimize works one-on-one with executives as a trusted sounding board, helping sharpen decision-making, uncover blind spots, and lead through change with greater confidence. This is not a program — it's a principal-led engagement built around your specific challenges, your team, and the outcomes that matter to you.

  • One-on-one executive leadership development
  • Uncovering blind spots and expanding leadership range
  • Burnout avoidance and recovery
  • Succession planning and organizational design
  • Change management and culture development
  • Growth planning and goal architecture
  • Time management and prioritization strategies
  • Talent acquisition and retention advisory
